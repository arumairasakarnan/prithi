About Sri Lanka

Learn about Sri Lanka's culture, history, and top travel tips.

Sri Lanka, known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a tropical island with diverse landscapes, ancient ruins, and vibrant culture. The country is famous for its tea plantations, wildlife sanctuaries, and beautiful beaches. Visitors can explore historic sites such as Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Polonnaruwa, and the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy. Sri Lanka is also a paradise for nature lovers, with national parks like Yala, Udawalawe, and Wilpattu offering opportunities to see elephants, leopards, and other wildlife up close. Whether you're looking for a relaxing beach holiday, an adventurous trek through the mountains, or a cultural tour of ancient temples, Sri Lanka has something for everyone.
